Guadalupe Reza is a fashion designer and an information designer.
With a visual and analytical perspective, Guadalupe Reza creates content about the fashion world. Her objective is to inform the situation, the influence and the people who are shaping the industry. IG: @guadalupereza TWTR: @guadalupe_reza
Her vision is to collaborate with fashion brands providing tools for data analysis and design strategies for sales growth, attraction of new markets and creation of new experiences.
She graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Clothing and Fashion Design from Ibero-american University (Dec 2017).
In 2017, she was a finalist of the National Design Award “Design Mexico” for her MXclilla Contemporánea project exhibited at the Franz Mayer Museum.
She co-founded Las Hijas del Jade in 2017, a jewelry brand made in Mexico, creating a tribe of irreverent and determined women: #LaTípicaChingona. Currently, she no longer participates in the brand.
He has worked since 2011 at Eva Accesorios, a wholesale retailer of accessories for women in Mexico. She has experience in sales and buying of product in China. She has also been involved in trend research, and on the creative side designing stands and advertising.
